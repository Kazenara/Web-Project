About Us

Polije Restaurant which was founded in 2018 by this Polytechnic Negeri Jember has a variety of menu's, ranging from Asian to western culinery. Polije Restaurant in addition to providing food, it also provides a variety of bobba drinks. The best selling food at this restaurant is beef steak, while the drink is matcha bobba.

Polije Restaurant prioritizes food and beverages that are processed and served hygienically, without using ingredients that are harmful to health. In addition, using environmentally friendly equipment, and buyers being responsible by disposing of their own trash after eating or drinking. So grab yours now!!
